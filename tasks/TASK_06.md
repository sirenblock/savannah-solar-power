### 4. How Tesla Powerwall 3 Works

**Normal Operation:**
1. Solar panels generate electricity
2. Powerwall 3's integrated inverter converts DC to AC
3. Your home uses solar power directly
4. Excess energy charges Powerwall
5. Powerwall powers home at night
6. Seamless grid connection for net metering

**During Outages:**
1. Grid failure detected in < 10 milliseconds
2. Powerwall instantly disconnects from grid
3. Powerwall powers your home
4. Solar continues operating (unlike Powerwall 2)
5. Solar recharges Powerwall during daylight
6. When grid returns, seamless reconnection

**Storm Watch Mode:**
When severe weather is detected, Powerwall automatically charges to 100% to prepare for potential outages.

### 10. Powerwall FAQs

**Can Powerwall power my whole home?**
Yes! Powerwall 3's 11.5 kW continuous power can run central AC, heating, and all appliances simultaneously.

**How long will Powerwall run my home?**
Depends on usage. Whole-home: 4-6 hours. Essential loads: 12-24 hours. With solar: indefinitely.

**Can I install multiple Powerwalls?**
Yes, up to 4 Powerwalls can be installed together (54 kWh total storage).

**Is Powerwall safe indoors?**
Absolutely. LFP chemistry (lithium iron phosphate) is extremely stable and safe for indoor installation.

**Does Powerwall work with my solar panels?**
Yes! Powerwall 3 works with any solar panels. Its integrated inverter supports up to 20 kW of solar.
