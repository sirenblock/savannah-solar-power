**Timeline:**

"Georgia law exempts solar systems from property tax assessments. Your home value increases, but property taxes don't."

Check current Georgia Power renewable energy programs (these change).

### 9. Financing FAQs

**Q: Can I use the tax credit as a down payment?**
A: No, the tax credit comes when you file taxes (following year). However, some lenders offer reduced payments in Year 1 assuming you'll apply tax credit.

**Q: What credit score do I need?**
A: Most solar loans require 650+ credit score. Some programs accept lower scores.

**Q: Are there prepayment penalties?**

**Q: What if I sell my house?**

### 5. What to Do Before a Hurricane

**72 Hours Before:**
- Charge battery backup to 100% (if applicable)
- Verify monitoring system is working
- Check rapid shutdown accessibility
- Clear debris from around panels
- Document system condition (photos)

**48 Hours Before:**
- Enable Storm Watch mode (Tesla Powerwall)
- Minimize battery usage to preserve charge
- Ensure critical load panel is configured correctly
- Have flashlights and emergency supplies ready

**24 Hours Before:**
- Final battery charge to 100%
- Note monitoring app status
- Know how to manually shut down system if needed

**Do NOT:**
- Attempt to remove panels yourself (dangerous and damages roof)
- Touch any electrical components during storm
- Work on roof in high winds

### 6. What to Do After a Storm

**Immediate Post-Storm:**
1. Check for visible damage from ground (never climb on roof in unsafe conditions)
2. Look for fallen branches, debris on panels
3. Check inverter status (lights, error codes)
4. Verify monitoring system is online

**If You See Damage:**
1. Take photos/video for documentation
2. Do NOT touch damaged electrical components
3. Contact us immediately for assessment
4. Contact your insurance company
5. Do not authorize repairs until insurance adjusts claim

**If System Appears Intact:**
1. Verify production has resumed (check monitoring app)
2. Watch for any performance anomalies
3. Schedule post-storm inspection for peace of mind

### 7. Common Storm Damage
